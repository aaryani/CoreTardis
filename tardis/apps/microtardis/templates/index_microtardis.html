Welcome to MicroTARDIS!

MicroTARDIS is a repository for microscopy research data at RMMF. When completed, it will automatically harvest data from frequently used instruments, securely storing it for your later retrieval. It will let you find data files by instrument settings, or specimen type if you entered it.

These instruments are currently being integrated:

  • FEI Quanta 200: .tif
    • EDAX detector: .spc
  • FEI Nova NanoSEM: .tif
    • EDAX detector: .spc

For more information on the MicroTARDIS project please contact eResearch Director Heinz Schmidt.
